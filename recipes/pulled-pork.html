BBQ Pulled Pork

Description

Delicious and tender slow cooked pulled pork made with root beer and topped with Sweet Baby Rays (or your favorite) BBQ sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 (2 pound) pork tenderloin
  • 1 (12 fluid oz) can of root beer
  • 1 (18 oz) bottle of your favorite BBQ sauce
  • 8 hamburger buns, lightly toasted

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Place pork tenderloin in a slow cooker;pour root beer over top.
  3. Cover and cook on low until pork shreds easily, 6 to 7 hours. Note: cooking times may vary.
  4. Drain well. Stir in barbecue sauce.
  5. Serve on hamburger buns.
